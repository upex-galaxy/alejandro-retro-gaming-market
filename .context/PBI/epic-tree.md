## Epic Hierarchy

### EPIC 1: Gestión de Cuentas y Perfiles
**Planned Jira Key:** EPIC-ARGM-001
**Priority:** CRITICAL
**Description:** Abarca el registro, inicio de sesión, perfiles de usuario y recuperación de contraseñas.

**User Stories (estimado: 4):**
1. STORY-ARGM-001 - As a new user, I want to be able to register on the platform with my email and a password to access the buying and selling features.
2. STORY-ARGM-002 - As a registered user, I want to be able to log in and out of my account securely to manage my activity on the platform.
3. STORY-ARGM-003 - As a registered user, I want to have a public profile where I can see my reputation, my items for sale (if I am a seller) and my purchases (if I am a buyer) to build trust in the community.
4. STORY-ARGM-004 - As a user who has forgotten my password, I want to be able to request a reset through my email to regain access to my account.

---

### EPIC 2: Marketplace: Compra y Venta
**Planned Jira Key:** EPIC-ARGM-002
**Priority:** CRITICAL
**Description:** Funcionalidades para crear, gestionar y buscar anuncios de productos.

**User Stories (estimado: 4):**
1. STORY-ARGM-005 - As a seller, I want to be able to create a listing for a retro console or game, uploading photos, a detailed description, price, and condition of the item, to be able to put my products up for sale.
2. STORY-ARGM-006 - As a seller, I want to be able to view and manage my active listings, being able to edit them or mark them as sold, to keep my inventory updated.
3. STORY-ARGM-007 - As a buyer, I want to be able to search and filter products by name, console, category, or condition to easily find the items that interest me.
4. STORY-ARGM-008 - As a buyer, I want to be able to see the detail page of a product with all the relevant information and the seller's profile to make an informed purchase decision.

---

### EPIC 3: Sistema de Confianza y Verificación
**Planned Jira Key:** EPIC-ARGM-003
**Priority:** HIGH
**Description:** Incluye la verificación de vendedores, el sistema de valoraciones y reseñas, y la gestión de disputas.

**User Stories (estimado: 4):**
1. STORY-ARGM-009 - As a new seller, I want to be able to go through a unique verification process (e.g. validating my identity) to obtain a "Verified Seller Seal" and thus generate more trust in buyers.
2. STORY-ARGM-010 - As a buyer, I want to be able to leave a rating (from 1 to 5 stars) and a review for a seller after a completed purchase to share my experience with the community.
3. STORY-ARGM-011 - As a seller, I want to be able to see the ratings and reviews I have received to build my reputation on the platform.
4. STORY-ARGM-012 - As a buyer or seller, if a problem arises with a transaction, I want to be able to open a dispute case so that the platform's support team can mediate and help me resolve it.

---

### EPIC 4: Comunidad y Descubrimiento
**Planned Jira Key:** EPIC-ARGM-004
**Priority:** MEDIUM
**Description:** Funcionalidades para ayudar a los usuarios a descubrir productos y conectar con la comunidad.

**User Stories (estimado: 2):**
1. STORY-ARGM-013 - As a novice buyer (Sofía), I want to access an interactive "Beginner's Guide" that asks me questions about my tastes in modern games to recommend an ideal retro console to start with.
2. STORY-ARGM-014 - As a buyer, I want to be able to read other users' reviews of a product or a seller to evaluate reliability and quality before buying.

---

### EPIC 5: Proceso de Pago Seguro
**Planned Jira Key:** EPIC-ARGM-005
**Priority:** CRITICAL
**Description:** Abarca todo el flujo de pago, desde el carrito de compra hasta el retiro de fondos por parte del vendedor.

**User Stories (estimado: 3):**
1. STORY-ARGM-015 - As a buyer, I want to be able to add a product to my shopping cart and proceed to payment securely through an integrated payment gateway to complete my order.
2. STORY-ARGM-016 - As a seller, I want to receive a notification when one of my products has been sold and the payment has been processed so I can proceed with shipping.
3. STORY-ARGM-017 - As a seller, I want the funds from my sales to accumulate in my platform account and be able to withdraw them to my bank account securely.
